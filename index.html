FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to import a car into Australia?+
Timeframes vary depending on approval type, origin country and compliance requirements. As a guide, vehicles from Japan typically take around 20–30 days at sea, while US imports run roughly 28–50 days door to door. We'll provide a realistic, tailored estimate during your quote.
Can any car be imported into Australia?+
No. Australian regulations are strict, and eligibility depends on the vehicle's age, build date and import pathway (such as the rolling 25-Year Rule, SEVS or the Personal Import Scheme). That's exactly why an eligibility assessment is the first step we handle — so you never ship a car that can't be registered.
Are there hidden costs?+
No. We provide transparent pricing and explain all potential costs upfront — shipping, customs duties, GST, quarantine fees, compliance and final delivery — so there are no nasty surprises down the line.
Do you handle everything?+
Yes — from approvals to shipping to customs, compliance and door-to-door delivery. With our own customs and quarantine depots in major Australian ports, the entire process is managed end-to-end by our in-house specialists.
